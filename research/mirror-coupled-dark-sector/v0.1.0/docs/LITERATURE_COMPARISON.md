## Series-level novelty assessment

The series does **not** introduce the general ideas of dark photons, self-interacting dark matter, asymmetric dark matter, or atomic dark matter. Its defensible novelty is the disciplined organization of a specific restricted hypothesis:

- define the equality at a renormalization scale;
- keep the broader hidden-sector model distinct from the equality;
- identify the scattering-regime failure of naive Born estimates;
- require one parameter point to survive dwarf, galaxy, cluster, cosmological, and portal constraints;
- state in advance what observations would falsify the restricted model.
