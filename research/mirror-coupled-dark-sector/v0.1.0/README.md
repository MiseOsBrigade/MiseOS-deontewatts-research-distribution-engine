# Mirror-Coupled Dark Sector Research Program

Version: **0.1.0**  
Release date: **2026-07-13**  
Author: **Deonte Watts**  
ORCID: **0009-0005-8586-3650**

A three-manuscript research package examining the conjecture that a hidden-sector gauge coupling may satisfy `alpha_D ~ alpha` while remaining consistent with dark-matter cosmology, velocity-dependent self-interaction, and dark-sector structure.

## Scientific status

The package distinguishes:

1. Established observations and standard calculations supported by cited literature.
2. Derived equations and scaling relations worked out in the manuscripts.
3. Original conjectures that remain unverified, especially `alpha_D = alpha` at a specified matching scale.

No manuscript claims that the equality has been measured or derived from an accepted ultraviolet theory.

## Manuscripts

1. **Mirror-Coupled Hidden Sectors** — gauge-theory construction, cold limit, asymmetric abundance, scattering regimes, and consistency conditions.
2. **Velocity-Dependent Self-Interactions in a Mirror-Coupled Dark Sector** — dwarf-to-cluster tests, halo scattering rates, baryon-halo response, cosmological constraints, and falsification matrix.
3. **Coupling Constants as Organizing Principles Across Physical Scales** — atomic and dark-atomic derivations, limits of cross-scale analogy, and discovery criteria.
